We are known for our delicious Italian meals and sushi platters, which all happen to be kosher.
Whether you’re heading in with a coworker or looking to strike up a conversation with a friendly new face,
Jade is the perfect spot to be. The restaurants and bars at the Jade make every meal time special. Fusing the essence of Italian cooking with cuisines from other cultures around the world, we invite our guests to traverse the culinary globe via the hotel’s collection of high quality restaurants and cafes.


Next to the hotel lobby, choose from a tantalising array of freshly baked breads, homemade cakes and pastries, light snacks and beverages including freshly squeezed juices, flavoured teas, speciality coffees and refreshing “mocktails”. The café and terrace is open throughout the day and evening for informal business meetings, family time or a relaxed catch up with friends. Our all new Wild Rice Restaurant, elegantly appointed on the lobby level of the hotel, celebrates the eclectic mix of flavours from China, Japan and Thailand. Choose from a wonderful selection of a la carte favourites at both lunch and dinner. Expert staff are always on hand to guide you on your discovery of these rich, exotic flavours.
